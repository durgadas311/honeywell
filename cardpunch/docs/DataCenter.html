Virtual IBM Punch Card Data Processing

This set of simulations is intended to demonstrate what data processing was like in the early decades of the computer era, prior to the arrival of affordable general-purpose computers. In many cases, some or all of these practices continued even at sites that had computers, partly because of established procedures but also because time on the computer was expensive and reserved for tasks that could not readily be handled by standard punch card data processing equipment.
